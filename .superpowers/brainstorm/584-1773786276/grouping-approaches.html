C — Labeled Sections

Small uppercase labels above each button group. Light and open feel. Labels double as screen reader landmarks. No heavy borders.

Note: These can be mixed — e.g., cards for major sections (description + nav) with labels for smaller button groups. Click the one that feels closest to what you want, and tell me in the terminal what you like or don't like.
