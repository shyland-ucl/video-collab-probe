What's Unique to Probe 3

In Probe 3, creator and helper each have their own phone (unlike Probe 2 where they share one device). So the helper needs:

  • Sync/Independent toggle — by default the helper's video follows the creator's position. "Independent Mode" lets the helper navigate freely.
  • Creator Activity feed — shows what the creator is doing on their device in real-time (e.g., "Viewing Scene 3", "Asked AI about lighting").

Option A: Each gets its own purple card — clearer but more scrolling.
Option B: Folded into existing cards — more compact but denser.
